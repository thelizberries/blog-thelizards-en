## What you find in the channel

No spam. No filling content. Only what matters:

- **new live dates*** and tour updates
- ** preview*** on outputs, singles and studio work

We chose WhatsApp because it is direct, personal and without too much ceremony. Like our rock.

If you're among those people, the channel is for you.

Thanks to those who follow us from before we had a name written on a flyer — and to those who arrived later, with the same intensity. Keep up.
