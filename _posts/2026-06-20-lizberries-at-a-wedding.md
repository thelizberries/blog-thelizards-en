The LIZBERRIES A MATRIMONY? Yes, it is


♪

Last Tuesday we had a special experience: for the first time in our history
We played a wedding!
Well yes, Vanessa and Fabio decided to celebrate their most beautiful day surrounded by everything
what represents their passion: Irish culture.
Obviously, in addition to druids, kilts and atmospheres from Emerald Island, a band could not miss
dedicated to their beloved Cranberries.
And that's how they chose us!


Between Emotions and SODDISFACTIONS

For the Lizberries it was an incredible experience: playing the day when two people
crown their dream of love is something unique. We felt a strong emotion and
so much hope to be up to this occasion.
Fortunately Vanessa and Fabio have reassured us: at the end of our performance we have
filled with compliments and told us how important it was for them to have music
of the Cranberries as a soundtrack of such an important moment.
We also felt a wonderful climate, made of enthusiasm and sincere love for
music.




We created a ladder suitable for the occasion: the rock spirit that distinguishes music
of the Irish band remained intact, but combined with the romantic atmosphere typical of that
Now.
The songs from the sweetest and most poetic rhythm have therefore been combined with the most energetic and wrinkled.




This was also a marriage of meetings and coincidences: Cristina has
found a childhood friend who hadn't seen for many years, and we also found the
our dear friend Marco B. Valsecchi together with his historical collaborator Marco dello
Shamrock of Lecco, committed to pin Guinness so much as to exhaust its stocks!
Music, old friends and new acquaintances have helped create an atmosphere really
special inside the Cascina Countryside residence of Pievedizio (BS).
But the true added value were the spouses: sympathetic, genuine and deeply “Irish” in
spirit.




We can therefore say that we have inaugurated the “marriage” format: if you also dream of
accompany your most beautiful day with the emotions, poetry and energy of music of
Cranberries, we will be happy to help you make it even more special.
Because every love story deserves its unforgettable soundtrack!
