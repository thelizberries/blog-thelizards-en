## The Lizards on Radio Deejay Fox: how it went

The Lizards were guests

<!--more-->

by Radio Deejay Fox on April 29, 2026 — and that studio, for one afternoon, became exactly the right place to be.

They have been playing original music since 2004. Four albums built in total autonomy. Since 2024 I have been with the Sorry Mom label — not a point of arrival, but the beginning of a new chapter.

We transformed all this specific weight into music.



## DJ Magnum, Mauro and Gigi: when the studio becomes the stage

They know rock — and it shows. They know when to speak and when to let the music fill the space.

They welcomed us with the same energy with which you welcome a band that has something to say.

You don't build certain atmospheres. You find them — and when you find them, you recognize them immediately.

## Strange Dream: twenty years of courage in one song

Strange Dream arrived in the studio with all its weight.

It's one of those Italian alternative rock songs that aren't easily explained — and that's not a flaw, it's its strength. It enters you without knocking, occupies a space you didn't know you had free and never leaves.

Twenty years of original music produces this: songs that know where they want to go. Energy, identity, courage: all in the same afternoon.

## The most complicated family tree of Italian rock

There is also the question of kinship. Cristina and Stefano and Alessandro are brothers. But Cristina is their third cousin: their grandfather was her uncle's cousin, her aunt's husband, her mother's sister.

Didn't you understand anything? Perfect. Sorry for the mess.

## Next stop: the tour of Ireland

There is a green island waiting for us. And this time we don't just go with other people's songs in our suitcase — we go with our own.

With those written at night, those born from a true emotion, those that even now, when we play them, make us feel that we are saying something necessary.

We will return to Radio Deejay Fox with something new to tell. A huge hug to all the staff: it is one of the most loved web radios in Italy, and every time we go up to that room we understand it again.
